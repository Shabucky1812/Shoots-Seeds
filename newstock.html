What's New?

Poppies

Brand new to our collection this month is the poppy! A timeless classic with symbolism for many, the poppy will fit into any garden or wild environment. Once established, poppies do not require lots of water to stay healthy so they may be a great choice for beginners.

Hyacinths

Another gorgeous new arrival to our stores is the hyacinth! These pretty plants stand out wherever they are located and are also relatively simple to maintain. We have a large selection of colours available so you can be sure the perfect hyacinth plant will be waiting for you in-store.

Crysanthemums

This month's final addition is the crysanthemum! We have these ready to be bought fully-grown or as seeds for those who can handle waiting just a little longer to scatter their garden with these lovely mums. Just make sure to keep the soil around your mums moist and remove any wilted flowers for maximum new growth.
