About Us

Who Are We?

We are Shoots & Seeds, your brand-new favourite retailer for all things plants! We're a local team of horticultural specialists who truly care about nature, gardening and, most importantly, you! We have collectively been in the business for over 30 years and we're proud to say that we are a team you can trust. So whether you're looking for your garden's latest feature piece or you're just interested to learn more, come say hi, we're always happy to help!

What Do We Do?

Our team is passionate about plants, from a tiny seed all the way to a towering tree! We grow all of our products from scratch, by hand, with love aplenty so that when you just can't get that one flower out of your head, you know where to go. Our available range cycles monthly so make sure to check in often to snatch all your favourites. We also sell an evergrowing collection of seeds and saplings all year round to guarantee that no budding gardener is ever stuck in the mud.

Why Choose Us?

We firmly believe in giving more than you take which is why we are happy to say we are officialy a carbon-negative business. By buying our plants, not only are you enjoying all that nature has to give, you're even helping to reverse the effects of global warming! Additionally, due to our partners all over the globe, we are able to grow and sell plants with restricted seasonal growth throughout the year.
